UX/UI Design

Escapes App

Escapes is a simple meditation mobile app that allows users take a travel in different relaxing and inspiring locations. The app helps users relax their mind through meditation music.

  • Design Research
  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
  • UX/UI Design
  • UI Animation

Challenge

After talking to the client about his vision and exploring similar apps in the store, we were focused on creating the relaxing and meditation feels in the app. Since the target audience is beginners, the design should not become too serious, on the contrary, it should give a sense of friendless, calmness and serenity, so we placed greater emphasis on the graphics that should convey the idea of the app.

Project Approach

Our main goal was to create a holistic picture, consisting of the separate of meditative sessions, which would complement each other at the expense of colors and interaction.

Color Scheme

Our approach initially directed us to ensure that the selected colors match the meditative music and convey a sense of calmness and serenity.

Visual Style

Each session is a reflection of some natural location, therefore to convey the atmosphere of each of them, we chose a monotone colors for each illustration in which the shades of the same color change in the game of gradients.

Icon Design

We created several concepts for the icon design, and the client chose this solution since the combination of the wave effect and gradient colors most accurately convey the association with meditative music.
