Brand Identity

Planime Studio

Planime studio creates animated videos for companies from different industries. It's a new firm that wants to be valuable and recognizable for their clients.

The Challenge

The client asked us to make the visual identity that could engage new clients, preparing the route for market entry. We started from the identification of the vision and the mission to arrive at the creation of a visual identity that should inherently be minimalistic, modern and fun.

Goals

  • Design a new brand identity.
  • Express the vision of a new firm.
  • Gain customer trust for new brand.

Logo Concept

The name of the company comes from "planimetrics" (the study of plane measurements, including angles, distances, and areas). The client wanted to incorporate a planet or comet into the logo. We took as a basis that the company creates animated videos, so using the "smile" element we created a minimal and fun logo that should be associated with the positive emotions we get when watching the animated videos.
